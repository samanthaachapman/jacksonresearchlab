Join the Lab

We welcome undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral students who are passionate about youth mental health, equity-based research, and reimagining systems of care.

Opportunities

We offer opportunities for independent studies, research assistantships, volunteer roles, and student-led projects. If you're interested in joining the lab, please reach out to Dr. Jackson directly at margotj@ualberta.ca.

Who We Work With

We collaborate with students from diverse disciplinary backgrounds, including nursing, public health, psychology, sociology, Indigenous studies, and digital health.

How to Get Involved

If you’re interested in exploring research or completing a course-based or thesis project with us, email Dr. Jackson with your CV, area of interest, and any relevant experience. We are always open to supporting new ideas that align with the lab’s goals.
