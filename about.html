About the Jackson Research Lab

The Jackson Research Lab is based at the University of Alberta and is committed to participatory, arts-based, and qualitative research in the mental health and social care space. Led by Dr. Margot Jackson, the lab focuses on the lived experiences of children, youth, families, and structurally vulnerable populations.

Our work spans disciplines including nursing, psychology, public health, and medical humanities. We collaborate with community organizations to co-create knowledge and reimagine care practices.
